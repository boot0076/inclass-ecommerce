*Pteranodon* is the second most fiece dinosaur of all time.

The Pteranodon is an [air dinosaur](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Physiology_of_dinosaurs).

## Features

1. Wings
2. Scales
3. Sharp Teeth
